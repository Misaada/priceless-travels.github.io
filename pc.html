Savour a starred experience
of food and wellness

The starred chef Daniele Usai is a member of JRE Italia, the prestigious international association of young restaurateurs.
His restaurant, Il Tino, and all the restaurants that are part of the JRE circuit, always have a surprise in store for Priceless Cities members who confirm their membership to the program when reserving their table.

In addition, for Mastercard cardholders who wish to explore additional delicacies, Usai has designed a unique program at the Nautilus Marina shipyard, where the River Tiber meets the Tyrrhenian sea.
The program, called “Il Tino and the Oasis”, begins with a relaxing herbal tea to welcome guests to the Oasis, the SPA adjacent to the restaurant, where guests can enjoy a complete wellness program including Reiki treatment.
Following this, guests are invited to a riverside aperitif, complete with sparkling wine and finger food before commencing a 7-course gastronomic experience, each dish perfectly paired to wines hand-picked by the chef from the cellar that features more than 200 wine labels.

If required, an upgrade to the program is available called “Il Tino, The Oasis and The Sea”, which also includes an aperitif at sea, on board one of the boats
